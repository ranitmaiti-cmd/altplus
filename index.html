The Studio

Where heritage
meets the now.

Alt Plus is a design-led luxury residential interior studio co-founded by Ranit Maiti and Subhrajit G. Mitra — architects with eighteen years of award-winning practice and a Forbes 30 Under 45 recognition.

We work with a curated roster of clients who value design not as decoration, but as an act of intention. Every home we create holds memory, reflects character, and appreciates over time.

Material Intelligence

Every surface selected for how it ages, feels, and what it says about the people who live with it.

Cultural Rootedness

Indian craft traditions inform every project — living language, never pastiche.

Absolute Discretion

Every commission treated as a private trust. Complete confidentiality at every stage.

End-to-End Creation

From first sketch to final installation — every detail managed with precision.
